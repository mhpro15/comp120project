Villa Solimar

VILLA SOLIMAR is a two-storey Mediterranean-style villa offering the ultimate in privacy and luxury.


With A double-height sky-lit foyer and wrought iron staircase, this villa gives a grand entrance where you will find a grandeur house accented with columns, door, and window trims, rendered in the coral finish, high ceiling, and white picked beams.


With glassed French doors, the sunlight radiates in the room, providing an amazing view of the pool and the Caribbean sea. The kitchen has classic cabinetry with quartz countertops. bar/breakfast area with a large pantry.


Each home features two bedrooms, two bathrooms, a great room, and fully-equipped kitchens. All doors and windows are storm impact, including French doors and sliding glass doors, and both homes have been outfitted with all-new roofs, wiring, electrical fixtures, and appliances


The family room & office/library has custom-built entertainment and in-wall cabinets, while the upper floor has 4 ensuite bedrooms. The master has a sitting area & TV armoire and his & hers walk-in closets, double sinks, and a delightful Jacuzzi tub.


Villa Solimar is equipped and furnished to the extremely highest standards and extremely pleasing to the eye inside as is outside.


This property was designed with comfort, style & elegance in mind.
