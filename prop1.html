Blue Lagoon Island

Blue Lagoon Island is a stunning 7-acre private island situated in a protected Lagoon in warm and welcoming Abaco in The Bahamas.


The perfect marriage of ultimate privacy and easy accessibility, this tranquil oasis overlooks a crystal-clear blue water lagoon brimming with vibrant sea life from baby turtles to a vast array of tropical fish making Blue Lagoon Island the ideal family escape for making new memories while sharing outdoor experiences such as snorkeling, paddle boarding, and spearfishing.


Nestled on either end of this lush, tropical cay are two well-built homes appropriately named The East End House, offering 1,774 sq. ft. of indoor space and 3,160 sq. ft. including the covered decks, and The West End House which offers 1,360 sq. ft. of indoor space and 2,630 sq. ft. including the covered decks.


Each home features two bedrooms, two bathrooms, a great room, and fully-equipped kitchens. All doors and windows are storm impact, including French doors and sliding glass doors, and both homes have been outfitted with all-new roofs, wiring, electrical fixtures, and appliances


There are also two docks on the island next to the East End House and a partial dock located near the West End House.


Blue Lagoon Island has approximately one mile of golf cart paths connecting the two homes on each end, and there are four plus acres of virtually untouched land between the two homes, leaving ample elevated space for future development of multiple cottages for guests or staff or an expansive private estate.


The remaining renovation potential includes completion of the kitchen in the West End House, landscape walls, completion of a third dock, customizing the cay's native landscaping, and building an extended dock with a rock protection wall on the Sea of Abaco to accommodate larger sea vessels.


More development potential exists on Scotland Cay as Blue Lagoon Island is being offered along with a lot on Scotland Cay which comes with a boat slip for a 40' boat.


A private community just east of Blue Lagoon Island, Scotland Cay also has a 3,200-ft. paved airstrip, thus making Blue Lagoon Island accessible by private plane.


Blue Lagoon Island Location Highlights: • 19 miles to historic Green Turtle Cay, a charming historic settlement. • 18 miles to Southworth Development’s award-winning Winding Bay. • 12 miles from Hope Town. • 6 miles to Man-O-War Cay, a historic settlement, known for its boat-making and sail craft. • 6 miles east of the new Montage development on Matt Lowes Cay. • 7 miles to Marsh Harbour International Airport. • 4 miles west is Discovery Land's Bakers Bay
